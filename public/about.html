Purpose of the System

The Faculty Appraisal System is a comprehensive digital platform designed to evaluate teaching staff fairly and consistently. It promotes academic excellence by providing meaningful feedback, fostering self-improvement, and aligning faculty goals with institutional objectives.

Key Features

  • ✅ Faculty self-assessments to reflect on performance
  • ✅ Anonymous student evaluations based on teaching effectiveness
  • ✅ Peer evaluations to ensure collaborative review
  • ✅ Admin-controlled question management and evaluation status tracking
  • ✅ Subject and class management for structured evaluation
  • ✅ Customizable questionnaires with rating systems
  • ✅ Dashboard analytics showing faculty, students, and user statistics

Why This System Matters

Transparency in performance evaluations is essential for institutional integrity. This system bridges gaps between students, faculty, and administrators by creating a clear, unbiased process for performance review. It helps institutions promote deserving faculty, identify improvement areas, and ensure quality education standards.

Who Can Use This Platform?

  • Students: Provide feedback on faculty members based on classroom experience.
  • Faculty: Review self-assessment and peer feedback to improve teaching methods.
  • Admin: Manage users, questions, evaluation status, and system statistics.
